## Product Description
HackRadar is a revolutionary AI-powered real-time hackathon evaluation platform that transforms how hackathons are judged and participated in. Our system automatically analyzes team submissions across multiple formats (PDFs, websites, screenshots, pitch decks) using sophisticated multi-agent AI evaluation, providing instant scoring, detailed feedback, and live leaderboards. Teams receive continuous guidance throughout the event, while organizers get comprehensive analytics and fair, consistent evaluation across all projects.

Built in under 12 hours during AGI Ventures Canada Hackathon 3.0, HackRadar demonstrates enterprise-level architecture with production-ready deployment at hackradar.me.
